🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect email addresses when you subscribe to job alerts. We also collect anonymous usage data such as page views and job clicks to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely to send job alerts you subscribed for. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management and analytics (Google Analytics if enabled). You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (a PostgreSQL database) with encryption and row-level security policies. We follow industry best practices.

5. Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from job alerts at any time by contacting us at admin@mycareerjob.in with your email address.

Job information on MyCareerJob.in is sourced from public sources and RSS feeds. Always verify details on official government or company websites before applying. We are not responsible for any recruitment decisions.
